Museums Worldwide

There are about 55,000 museums worldwide! Museums can have several kinds of themes: art, history, science, or combinations of several different subject matters.

The American Alliance of Museums is a great resource to explore the world of museums and all they have to offer!

Notable Art Museums

The Louvre (Paris, France), The Museum of Modern Art (New York City, New York), The State Hermitage Museum (St. Petersburg, Russia), Tate Modern (London, England), and Museo Nacional Del Prado (Madrid, Spain)

Notable History Museums

Acropolis Museum (Athens, Greece), Hagia Sophia (Istanbul, Turkey), The Army Museum (Paris, France), The Asian Civilizations Museum (Singapore), and The Egyptian Museum (Cairo, Egypt)

Notable Science Museums

The Smithsonian Institute (Washington D.C.), The Natural History Museum (London, England), The Exploratorium (San Francisco, California), Te Papa Museum (Wellington, New Zealand), and The Universeum (Sweden)

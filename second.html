My Work at the Museum of Life and Science

The Museum of Life and Science is located in Durham, North Carolina. My work at the museum is varied; I lead programs focused in several different fields of science, but primarily, my program focuses are paleontology and zoology. Here's a link to a video of me leading a virtual program on dinosaurs!

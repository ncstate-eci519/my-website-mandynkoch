My Work at the Museum

The Museum of Life and Science

The Museum of Life and Science is located in Durham, North Carolina. My work at the museum is varied; I lead programs focused in several different fields of science, but primarily, my program focuses are paleontology and zoology.

Museum of Life and Science Homepage

Birthday Parties

My main role at the Museum is managing and developing programming for children's birthday parties. We offer seven different themed parties, all revolving around different scientific fields. We also started offering virtual birthday parties in light of the COVID-19 pandemic.

Here's a link to the Museum's birthday party homepage!

Other Odd Jobs

At the Museum, I also serve as the Animal Ambassador Trainer, which means I'm responsible for training all of the program animal handlers. These handlers take animals out on public programs, such as field trips, school groups, birthday parties, or even weddings!
